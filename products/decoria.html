Decoria, is derived from Bohus BioTech’s more than 20 years of experience from research, development and manufacturing of hyaluronic acid-based products. The manufacturing process of Decoria is itself based on 30 years of Swedish research and is a unique advancement of the filler manufacturing process, creating a product that is both safe, durable and easy to handle.

  • Innovative spherification technology
  • High molecular hyaluronic acid
  • Made in Sweden
  • Extra long-lasting result

The Decoria product range offers four products for aesthetic enhancement of the face. Decoria: Essence, Intense, Voluma and Lips. Decoria consists of a stabilized and highly purified form of hyaluronic acid extracted from a synthetic source, structurally identical to the body’s own hyaluronic acid, creating a highly biocompatible product. They are injectable implants, in the form of a transparent gel, used for correction and shaping of facial wrinkles and folds, facial contouring and for lip enhancement. Just one injection of Decoria produces an immediate result and a long-lasting effect.

The hyaluronic acid in Decoria has been stabilized to give an extra-long-lasting result. The products provides a significant aesthetic improvement with a duration of 6-12 months, depending, among other things, on the area treated and the structure of the skin.

Decoria Essence

Decoria Essence® is intended for the correction and shaping of fine lines and superficial wrinkles. The product may also be used for fine corrections of the lips, and to mark the edges of the lips, or to create a distinct Cupid’s bow.

The product should be injected intradermally through a needle into the superficial to mid dermis.

Decoria Intense

Decoria Intense® is recommended for the treatment of deep lines, wrinkles, imperfections and for jaw, cheek and nose enhancement.

The product should be injected intradermally through a needle into the mid to deep layers of the dermis.

Decoria Voluma

Decoria Voluma® is preferably used for deep wrinkles and folds as well as for jaw, cheek and nose enhancement.

The product should be injected intradermally through a needle into the deep layers of the dermis.

Decoria Lips

Decoria Lips® is intended to restore and/or augment lip volume. The product should be injected into the submucosal layer.

Please note that we recommend patients planning to undergo a treatment with any of our dermal fillers to control that the practitioner performing the treatment is certified according to national legislation, rules or common certification, for example “Estetiska injektionsrådet” in Sweden.
